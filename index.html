Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Focuses on holistic development through play-based learning. Key areas include language, numeracy, motor skills, creativity, and social-emotional growth. Activities like storytelling, counting, art, and nature exploration build curiosity, confidence, and foundational life skills.

Middle (10-16) Years

Focuses on academic depth, critical thinking, and personal development.
